Important Note:

  • Customer Satisfaction:
    Our goal is to ensure your complete satisfaction with our printing services. If you have any concerns or questions throughout the process, please don't hesitate to reach out to our customer service team. We are here to assist you and address any issues promptly.
  • Proofing:
    It's crucial to carefully review the proof provided before giving final approval for printing. Check for any errors, typos, or design discrepancies. Once approved, changes may not be possible, and any reprints may incur additional costs.
  • Artwork Submission:
    Please ensure that the artwork files are high-resolution and meet our specified requirements (e.g., file format, bleed, color mode). If you need assistance with artwork preparation, please contact our team for support.

At Creative Project, we want to ensure a smooth and successful printing experience for our valued customers. Please keep the above mentioned reminders in mind.
